Who am I?



Hello all, thank you for taking the time to get to know me a bit better! My name is Peter Astle, I am am a software developer looking to transition into the tech industry after holding a myriad of positions within a variety of industries. After highschool I decided to pursue a degree in Business Management through Eastern Washington University and though I was unable to complete this degree due to non-academic reasons, I benefited from in-depth coursework with focus primarily on Economics, Management, and Business Statistics.

After my time studying at EWU, I began to search for a career which would bring new daily challenges, continual improvement opportunities, and personal fulfillment. Throughout this search I have held positions such as an assembly line production worker building Toyota Highlanders, a marketing copywriter creating descriptions for newly released products and formatting each using HTML for direct uploads to the business' online storefront, and most recently as an Auto Claims Representative for Liberty Mutual determining liability for auto claims and managing the repair resolutions for each party throughout the claims lifecycle.

While none of these roles involked a passion within me to progress further with those roles, each provided me with ample learning opportunities and helped me discover which aspects of a position produce the personal fulfillment which I am seeking in a career. I concluded that my passion for efficiency, process improvement, and helping others through finding and delivering creative solutions to satisfiy their unique needs.

This realization helped drive my decision to take on the challenge of transitioning into a software developer role by means of education/training through ElevenFifty Academy where I learned the fundamentals of being a full-stack software developer. In this project focused coursework I have been able to work with HTML, CSS, C#, and a bit of Javascript. Below you will find a small showcase of the body of work which I have managed to complete since I began this journey, which I hope will accurately display my current skill level in addition to the abundant growth which ElevenFifty has facilitated over the past couple of months.
